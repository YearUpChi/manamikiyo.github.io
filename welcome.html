What is Magic The Gathering?

Magic: The Gathering (also known as either MTG or Magic) is a trading card game created by Richard Garfield. First published in 1993 by Wizards of the Coast, Magic was the first trading card game produced and it continues to thrive, with approximately twenty million players as of 2015 (Source). This ever evolving strategy game evolves research into some of the lore and what deck is the best for you.
